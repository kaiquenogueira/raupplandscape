What We Do

Six disciplines, one standard.

01

Lawn Care & Maintenance

Weekly mowing, edging and fertilization programs that keep your turf dense, deep-green and disease-resistant from April through November.

02

Garden Design & Planting

Custom planting plans tuned to your soil and exposure — perennials, native shrubs and seasonal color that mature into a layered, four-season landscape.

03

Hardscaping & Patios

Bluestone patios, paver walkways, retaining walls and stone edging built to last decades. We obsess over drainage, base prep and clean lines.

04

Landscape Lighting

Low-voltage LED systems that draw the eye to specimen trees, façades and pathways — extending every garden well past sunset.

05

Snow Removal

24/7 storm response across West Hartford and surrounding towns. Driveways, walkways and entries cleared, salted and ready before sunrise.

06

Seasonal Cleanup

Spring openings and fall cleanups done right — leaf removal, bed cutbacks, dethatching and winter prep that protect your investment year over year.
